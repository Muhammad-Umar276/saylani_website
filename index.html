Maulana Bashir Ahmed Farooqi is the founder and chairman of Saylani Welfare International Trust, a renowned charitable organization in Pakistan. Under his leadership, Saylani has been actively working to alleviate poverty by providing free meals, healthcare, education, and vocational training to millions. With a vision to uplift society, he has initiated various projects, including an ambitious goal to produce 10 million skilled graduates to boost Pakistan’s economy. His efforts have been recognized at national levels, including discussions with leaders like President Dr. Arif Alvi, highlighting his dedication to humanitarian causes..
